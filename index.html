Mente Sabia Crú

brief history

Mente Sabia Crú is a Chilean hip hop group. They started off in Santiago, the capital city of Chile. The group was founded in 1999, but didn't start making music until the early 2000s. They're known for having sociually conscious lyrics, and a unique style at the time.

members

there are a total of five members in the group. These are,

  • Matiah Chinaski
  • Terrible
  • Dr.Bene
  • MC Unabez
  • DJ Pérez

Their group has collaborated with other Chilean artists and groups. One of these groupsis called Salvaje Decibel, which have a similar style to Mente Sabia Crú. Some members of Mente Sabia Crú have alos started their own solo careers. Members like Matiah Chinaski, Terrible, and Dr Bene have all released their own albums, in which they feature eachother and some other artists as well, such as Chystemc and Movimiento Original. some of their best albums are,

  • salven a las palomas - Dr.Bene
  • el tostador - Matiah Chinaski
  • No Me Olvides Tan Pronto - Mente Sabia Crú
  • Bitacore - Mente Sabia Crú
